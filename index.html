About me

I graduated with a PhD in Communication from Web Mining Lab in City University of Hong Kong in Feburary 2015.

After graduation, I worked at Openrice and Tencent as data scientist.

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Communication at the Shenzhen University. My research interests include computational advertising, ethics of algorithm, and bibliometrics. I have published papers in leading journals such as the Journal of Advertising Research, the EPJ Data Science, and the Scientometrics. I have taught courses on computational advertising and Python programming at both undergraduate and graduate levels.
